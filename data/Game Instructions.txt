*********Objective of the game******

Collect all the coins in the game world and move to the next level
by moving to the safe space which is the green area.

The enemies are disguised as coins therefore if you come in contact 
with an enemy, you will lose a life.

*********Movement*********

To move UP use w
To move DOWN use s
To move to the RIGHT use d
To move to the LEFT use a

To collect a coin, move to it
